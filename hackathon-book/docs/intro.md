# Welcome to "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics"

This textbook will guide you through the exciting world of **AI-driven humanoid robotics**, bridging the gap between digital AI and real-world physical intelligence.

## What You Will Learn

- Principles of **Physical AI** and embodied intelligence  
- Robot control using **ROS 2**  
- Simulations with **Gazebo & Unity**  
- AI-powered perception and navigation using **NVIDIA Isaac**  
- Voice-to-action commands with **LLMs & Whisper**  
- Designing humanoid robots for **natural interactions**  

## Book Structure

- **Module 1:** Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)  
- **Module 2:** Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)  
- **Module 3:** AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)  
- **Module 4:** Vision-Language-Action (VLA)  
- **Capstone Project:** Autonomous Humanoid Robot  

## How to Use This Book

- Each chapter contains **explanations, code examples, and exercises**  
- Interactive **RAG chatbot** (coming soon) will answer your questions based on this book  
- Recommended hardware for labs and simulations is included in later chapters
